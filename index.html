About Me

Building intelligent systems

Kirankumar Ashokkumar

Systems Engineer at Hyve Solutions

📧 💻 💼

I have been a Systems Engineer at Hyve Solutions for over two years, where my primary responsibility is designing and coding automation software based on Python and Kubernetes for manufacturing processes. I have also worked on various projects that help debug a wide range of hardware issues on servers. One of these projects is the Debug Genie automation chatbot, which takes an error message or question as input and provides a step-by-step guide for troubleshooting. Additionally, I built a robotic arm that travels to pre-programmed locations to analyze faulty cable connections using the ResNet152 model. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering from UC Merced, along with a minor in Applied Mathematics.

In my spare time, I enjoy working on projects related to embodied AI, which involves integrating machine learning techniques into physical robotic hardware. This field encompasses various subfields, including Natural Language Processing (NLP), Computer Vision, Reinforcement Learning, Navigation, Physics-Based Simulations, and Robotics. To advance my goal of becoming an engineer in this area, I have undertaken several projects that explore these concepts. You can find details about these projects in the project section.
